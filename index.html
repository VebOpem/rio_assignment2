I met a child a year ago Whose eyes would never see. She asked me with a timid smile,

"What colour is a tree?"

"In summertime a tree is green;

In autumn gold and red;

In winter they are frosted white when all their leaves are shed."

"I know the sky is blue," she said.

"And silver is the sand;

And apples are the brightest red.

What colour is a man?"

"Man is many colours child: Some are yellow, some are brown. And some are black as sightlessness, Some white as eiderdown."

She took her crayons from a box And placed them in my glove And said, "By mixing all of these Comes my favourite colour

love.
